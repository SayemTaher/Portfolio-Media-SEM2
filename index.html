Semester Objectives Explained...

This semester promises to be a interesting journey of discovery and growth in the field of Media Design. We will delve into the many facts of Media Design, from the traditional techniques of graphic design to the cutting-edge technology of film production and video editing. Along the way, we will build on our foundational knowledge of industry-standard tools like Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Media Encoder. With these tools, we will learn how to bring our creative visions to life, from designing logos to creating stunning visuals for film and video projects. The highlight of this semester, however, will be a group project where we will have the opportunity to work with a real-life client. This project will challenge us to put our newfound knowledge and skills to the test as we collaborate to create a product that meets the client's specific needs. In the process, we will be exposed to the many challenges that come with working on real-world projects, including the use of scripting languages such as JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. In short, this semester promises to be a fascinating exploration of the world of Media Design, where we will gain valuable hands-on experience, build our skills, and grow as professionals.
